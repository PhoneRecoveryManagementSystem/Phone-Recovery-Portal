## 🔍 Overview
The **Phone Recovery Portal** is a centralized platform designed to help individuals and organizations track, report, and recover lost or stolen mobile devices. By combining real-time tracking, photo evidence submission, and a user‑friendly interface, the portal streamlines the recovery process and increases the chances of getting devices back to their owners.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **📍 Phone Tracking** – Submit IMEI numbers and get location updates (integration with carrier APIs / GPS data).
- **📸 Photo Evidence** – Upload photos of the lost/stolen device, serial numbers, or even suspected locations to assist authorities.
- **📋 Recovery Portal** – A clean dashboard for users to view the status of their reports, communicate with recovery agents, and manage cases.
- **🔐 Secure & Private** – Data is encrypted and only accessible to authorized parties.
- **📱 Responsive Design** – Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
